What Causes Tooth Discoloration?

Tooth stains can be extrinsic (on the surface) or intrinsic (inside the tooth structure). Common culprits include:

  • Coffee, tea, and red wine
  • Tobacco and nicotine use
  • Certain medications
  • Poor oral hygiene
  • Natural aging process
